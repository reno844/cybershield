FAQ

Your security questions answered.

Quick guidance on how to stay safe without getting overwhelmed.

Look for misspellings, mismatched sender addresses, urgent requests, and unexpected attachments. When unsure, navigate directly to the website rather than clicking links.

Public Wi-Fi is convenient but often insecure. Avoid sensitive tasks like banking unless you use a trusted VPN or a secure personal hotspot.

A strong password is long, unique, and hard to guess. Prefer passphrases like a sentence or a combination of unrelated words, and store them in a password manager.
